Beyond the Stats


  • Faction balancing: Use data to highlight imbalances or trends, then ask why they're happening — not just what is happening.
  • Map design insights: Stats can show which maps are played more and hint at what makes them enjoyable or avoidable, helping creators improve design.
  • Probability and outcomes: Data can uncover patterns or unexpected outcomes, but probability doesn’t guarantee results — it frames them.
  • Informed design and strategy: Stats don't dictate decisions but provide context to explore better ones.
  • Deeper discussions: Let stats spark questions, challenge assumptions, and fuel creative problem-solving.
